# MediSecure - Potential Business Services

## Core Healthcare Services

### 1. Appointment Management System
Schedule, reschedule, and cancel appointments with doctors. Includes automated reminders and waitlist management.

### 2. Electronic Health Records (EHR) Management
Store and manage complete patient medical history including diagnoses, treatments, lab results, and imaging reports.

### 3. Prescription Management System
Digital prescription creation with automatic drug interaction checks and pharmacy integration for refills.

## Patient Care Services

### 4. Medication Reminder System
Send automated reminders to patients to take their medications on time and track adherence.

### 5. Telemedicine Service
Enable virtual doctor consultations through video calls with session recording and digital prescriptions.

### 6. Health Analytics Dashboard
Track patient vital signs, provide health trend analysis, and generate personalized health recommendations.

### 7. Medical Document Processing
Upload and process medical documents like lab reports and certificates with automatic data extraction.

## Communication Services

### 8. Multi-Channel Notification System
Send appointment reminders, test results, and alerts via email, SMS, and push notifications.

### 9. Patient-Doctor Messaging
Secure messaging between patients and healthcare providers with file sharing capabilities.

## Financial Services

### 10. Billing & Payment Processing
Generate invoices, process payments, and manage payment plans for medical services.

### 11. Insurance Claim Management
Submit and track insurance claims electronically with automated processing.

## Compliance & Security Services

### 12. HIPAA Compliance Monitoring
Monitor and log all access to patient data to ensure regulatory compliance and detect breaches.

### 13. Consent Management System
Record and manage patient consent for treatments, data sharing, and research participation.
